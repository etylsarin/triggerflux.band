Triggerflux

Bio

Triggerflux is a high-voltage electro-clash unit built on raw energy, dirty disco attitude, and ferocious live presence. Formed on the fringes of Adelaide's underground rave scene, the band fuses breakbeat chaos, layered interlocking guitar hooks, live drums, and destructive club sonics into a singular explosive identity.

Fronted by Mara Keaton, Triggerflux channels the sweat of warehouse raves and the defiance of those who refuse to be silenced.

Debut Album

Break the Silence (2025)

Twelve tracks of electro-clash fury and dirty disco swagger. From the creeping tension of "Voltage Drop" to the melancholic orbit of "Satellite Heart," Break the Silence maps the territory between rave euphoria and gritty nocturnal glamour.

The album opens with dark tension before exploding into anarchic defiance. It descends through nocturnal cityscapes — ghost frequencies, mechanical disco swagger, magnetic pulls. The heart of the album beats in its contrasts: sassy electro-clash swagger sits alongside dark electro-pop disillusionment; desperate dancefloor highs fade into hollow comedowns.

Track Listing: Voltage Drop • Riot Soul • Shockwave • Dead Signal • Iron Pulse • Magnet Run • Velvet Static • Glass Highway • Lipstick Traces • Hollow Crown • Lifted • Satellite Heart
